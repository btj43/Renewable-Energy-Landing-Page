Burning fossil fuels is the main contributor of greenhouse gas emissions. This is why the transistion towards renewable energy (which doesn't require the burning fossil fuels) is so urgent to create a sustainable future.
The use of renewable energy also helps prevent other negative impacts that the fossil fuel industry can cause, such as water pollution from coal mining, the potential of oil spills, damage to land in search of fossil fuels, e.g. fracking.
